## 4. Market Frustrations (from buyer research)

Top complaints about paid traffic agencies, ranked by frequency:

1. Zero results despite significant spend
2. Vanity metrics instead of real outcomes
3. Bait-and-switch (senior pitches, junior delivers)
4. Don't understand the client's niche
5. Black box operations / no transparency
6. Contract lock-ins / data hostage
7. Set-and-forget after month 2
8. Poor communication
9. Broken retainer model ("paying rent")
10. Over-promising, under-delivering

### What they wish they had

- Results-tied compensation
- Industry-specific expertise
- A single accountable person
- Month-to-month flexibility
- Full account ownership
- Plain-language reporting
- Strategic thinking, not just execution

### Pricing expectations (buyer side)

- Management fees: GBP 500-3,000/month expected
- GBP 2,000-5,000 total monthly (management + spend) comfort zone
- Expect results within 60-90 days
- Preferred model: flat fee or hybrid (base + performance)

Note: JGL's Expert Growth Engine at GBP 5,000/month is above this comfort zone. This is deliberate positioning - it targets established businesses (GBP 30k+/month) not startups. The price itself becomes a qualifier.
